Chocolate-Dipped Almond Cookies

Ingredients

  • For the Dough
    • 1 1/2 Cups Vegetable Shortening
    • 1 1/4 Cups Sugar
    • 1 Egg
    • 2 teaspoons Almond Extract
    • 3 Cups Flour
    • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
    • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • For the Coating
    • 8 Ounces Semi-Sweet Chocolate (melted)
    • 1 1/2 Cups Finely-Chopped Sliced Almonds

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  2. In large mixing bowl, cream shortening and sugar. Using a silicone spatula, scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl.
  3. Mix in egg and almond extract until combined. Scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl.
  4. In separate bowl, blend flour, baking soda and salt.
  5. With the mixer running on slow, add dry ingredients to the creamed mixture and mix until well combined. Scrape down the sides and bottom of the bowl.
  6. Shape dough into 1-inch balls; place on parchment paper-lined baking sheets. Slightly flatten dough balls with the bottom of a glass.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Cookies will not change in color when done.
  8. Cool on baking sheets for 2 to 3 minutes; transfer to wire cooling racks to cool completely
  9. Roll each cookie along edge into melted chocolate, then almonds. Place on waxed paper-lined baking sheets; refrigerate until chocolate is set.
